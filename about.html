All about us..

Pet Emporium offers services such as pet grooming, hotel, and vet consultation.

Our mission..

is to provide only the best care to our four-legged guests and their owners. Having spacious accommodations and friendly staff awaiting your fur babies' arrival, you can rest assured that your furry family member will be in excellent hands while you are away. If you are looking for the perfect home away from home for your dog or cat near NAIA in Pasay or Clark Airport in Pampanga, you’ve come to the right place!

Pet Emporium Care & Hotel difference..

We understand that pets are family. Your decision to entrust us with your beloved companion’s care is not something we take lightly. We will show your pet the same love and respect that we show our own pets, and we will do everything possible to make sure they enjoy their time with us. Unlike many other pet care facilities, we welcome all pets regardless of their age, size, breed, or temperament. And we take the time to get to know each guest to learn their quirks. We are especially mindful of timid, fearful, and anxious pets and do everything we can to make them comfortable. By offering both small play groups and one-on-one play sessions with our kennel attendants, we can meet the needs of pets with various personality types.
